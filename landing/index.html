D Diffinity

See what changed, not just which pixels moved.

Semantic DOM diffing for visual regression testing.

Pixel-diff tools flag every changed pixel — a 1px border tweak and a full layout reflow look identical. Diffinity captures the DOM tree with computed styles, matches elements across before/after states, and tells you what changed in human terms: "Header background changed from #1a1a2e to #2d2d44 (major)" instead of "312 pixels differ."

The three scenarios below walk through the shapes of change diffinity is built to handle: a targeted CSS fix, a structural refactor that's visually a no-op, and a full rebrand that cascades across an app.
